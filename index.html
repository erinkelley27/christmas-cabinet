We LOVE the Christmas story! Which is why we created The Christmas Cabinet! The Christmas Cabinet is an Advent Calendar that creates a nativity scene. It is an heirloom that can be passed from generation to generation, creating Christmas memories that will last a lifetime and beyond. Starting December 1, open each day’s numbered drawer, one per day until December 24th, Christmas Eve. Each drawer holds a figure that represents the Christmas story, everything from genuine frankincense to the reproduction of a Roman statue of Caesar Augustus, as well as angels, little lambs and even John the Baptist and his parents. Read the story on the bottom of the drawer or from the included book. Arrange the 25 figures from the drawers to complete your own nativity scene and watch the greatest story ever told unfold!


Don’t worry of you lose one of the figures, you can order a replacement part here. Believe me, we understand! It happens. When my youngest was two, he flushed baby Jesus down the toilet. Unfortunately he was never the same again...my son that is, not Jesus!


Want to find out more? Use the “Going Deeper” tab to discover historical details pertaining to the story or the “Music” tab to find songs that relate to each of the 24 figures.
